Edge %
Edge = (Season Avg − Prop Line) / Prop Line × 100
Measures how much a player's season average exceeds (or falls short of) the prop line. A positive edge means the model average is above the line — lean Over. A negative edge means below the line — lean Under.
Example: Avg 28.4, Line 26.5 → Edge = (28.4−26.5)/26.5×100 = +7.2%
Hit Rate
Hit Rate = Games cleared line / Total games × 100
The percentage of recent games where the player actually went Over the prop line. This is the raw historical frequency — the platform's Model Probability.
Example: Cleared 26.5 pts in 7 of last 10 games → Hit Rate = 70%
Model Probability
Model Prob = Hit Rate / 100
ValueAlert's estimate of the probability the player goes Over, derived from historical hit rate. This is our view of the true probability based on real game data — independent of what the bookmaker thinks.
70% hit rate → Model Prob = 0.70 (70%)
Implied Probability
Positive odds: 100 / (odds + 100) Negative odds: |odds| / (|odds| + 100)
The probability the bookmaker is pricing into the odds. This includes their vig (margin), so implied prob for both sides adds up to more than 100%. It reflects market consensus — sharp money and public action combined.
-130 odds → 130/(130+100) = 56.5% implied
Expected Value (EV)
EV = (ModelProb × Payout) − (1 − ModelProb) × 100
The theoretical profit or loss per $100 wagered, given our model probability vs the offered odds. Positive EV means the bet is mathematically profitable long-term. The bookmaker's vig means most standard bets have negative EV — a positive EV edge is rare and valuable.
Model 70%, -130 odds → EV = (0.70×76.9) − (0.30×100) = +23.8%
Confidence (0–100)
Conf = Market Edge (0–40) + Hit Rate (0–40) + Consistency (0–20)
A composite score rating how trustworthy the edge signal is. Built from 3 independent components: EV edge (model vs implied prob gap), hit rate signal (deviation from 50%), and consistency (inverse of coefficient of variation).
70+ = High confidence · 50–69 = Moderate · <50 = Low
Value Score
Value = |Edge|×0.25 + max(0,HitRate−50)×0.25 + Confidence×0.25 + EV×0.25
A single composite ranking blending edge, hit rate, confidence, and EV equally. Players with Value Score ≥30, Edge >4%, Confidence ≥55, and EV >0 earn the ⚡ VALUE badge and appear in the Value Alerts tab.
Score ≥ 40 = Strong · 25–39 = Moderate · <25 = Risky
Lean
OVER if Edge > 4% or EV > 5% · UNDER if Edge < −4% or EV < −5%
The Over/Under recommendation based on edge and EV combined. The track position shows strength of the lean — further right = stronger Over signal, further left = stronger Under signal.
+8% edge → OVER ▲ · −6% edge → UNDER ▼ · ±2% → EVEN
¼ Kelly Criterion
f* = (b×p − q) / b · ¼ Kelly = f* × 0.25
The mathematically optimal bet size that maximizes long-term bankroll growth. b = net decimal payout, p = model probability, q = 1−p. We use Quarter Kelly (25% of full Kelly) for conservative risk management — full Kelly is aggressive and can cause large drawdowns.
Model 60%, +100 odds → f* = 20% → ¼ Kelly = 5% of bankroll
